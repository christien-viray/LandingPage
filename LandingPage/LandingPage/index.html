Meet Your Instructor

Cheska Viray is a Mississauga-based piano teacher, and travels to students homes to teach piano ranging from RCM Prep A to RCM Level 7.

She pursued her Bachelors of Music Education degree (BMus) from Western University, studying under Tina Yanchus-Hibbard, and completing her degree in 2021. She returned to Western University to complete her second degree in Primary/Junior Education (BEd) in 2023.

Cheska provides performance techniques, body awareness advice, and music theory lessons to help her students become a well rounded pianist. She is experienced in preparing her students for practical examinations with high quality results and provides performance opportunities with recitals. She recently hosted her studio's piano recital with great success, and is currently planning more performance opportunities for years to come.
